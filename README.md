Project Overview: "This tool automates the analysis of clinical trial datasets to evaluate the efficacy of a new drug (Drug-X) against a placebo in reducing Blood Pressure."

Key Features:

Automated calculation of BP Reduction across patient groups.

Statistical comparison of treatment outcomes.

Safety monitoring by tracking Adverse Effects.

Visual representation of efficacy using Seaborn Boxplots.

Why it matters: "In Clinical Data Management (CDM), such automation reduces manual error and speeds up the 'Database Lock' process."
